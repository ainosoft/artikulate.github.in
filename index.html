AI driven article extraction platform

Welcome to Artikulate - an AI driven article extraction platform deployed to fetch, convert, sort and display newspaper articles from various online sources in a well-formatted, tabulated and searchable format.

Using Machine learning algorithms trained on various online print formats, artikulate can extract articles with 99.99 percent accuracy at the rate of a million pages per hour with zero downtime

Artikulate has an in-built failure and retrial mechanism ensuring availability of your news articles with minimum human intervention. and supports manual upload of PDFs, ensuring all your business needs are met.
